About TST

Who We Are

TST (Technology Society Team) is an independent youth tech community dedicated to fostering innovation and creativity among young tech enthusiasts in the Delta region.

As the first startup company responsible for organizing tech events in our region, we're committed to creating opportunities for learning, networking, and growth in the technology sector.

We believe in the power of collaboration and community-driven innovation to shape the future of technology.

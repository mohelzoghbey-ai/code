Our Mission

TST (Technology Society Team) is an independent youth tech community dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and collaboration among tech enthusiasts in the Delta region.

We are pioneering the startup ecosystem in our region by organizing cutting-edge tech events, workshops, and hackathons that bring together passionate individuals to learn, build, and grow together.

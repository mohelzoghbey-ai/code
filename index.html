About TST

TST (Technology Society Team) is an independent youth tech community that aims to build a space for creative and tech-passionate individuals.

1

We believe that every student or beginner has the potential to start their journey in the world of technology.

2

Through our events, workshops, and online content, we help young people explore various tech fields.

3

Our vision is to create a youth tech community in Egypt that inspires, empowers, and provides real opportunities.
